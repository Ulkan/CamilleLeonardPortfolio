About

I'm currently studying for a game design bachelor at ICAN. I enjoy participating in Game Jams and attending masterclasses related to video games. I have an interest for new technologies, in particular : Virtual Reality, Alternate Reality and custom controllers.

I wish to work as a Level Designer for action and adventure games. I like to build level that the player want to play and who tell us story. I am interested in working for party game or serious game because it can help people to socialize or get good habits.

Crashing Bunnies

In Crashing Bunnies, your goal is to defeat other players by creating shockwaves, while avoiding theirs by jumping over them !
The longer you stay airborne, the bigger the shockwave will be.
Up to 4 players can play together.

  • GGJ page: Crashing Bunnies GGJ page
  • Date: January 20 - 22 2017
  • Role: Game Design, Graphism, Animation, Arena Building

Orbit

Orbit is an athmospheric game where the player moves in a sphere in which there are 4 colored lights distributed equidistantly to the surface. The player create crystals and must balance their distribution in the sphere to prevent a black hole from destroying them.

  • GOD : Download Game Overview Document
  • Game Prototype: Download Orbit.exe
  • Date: Octobor - January 2016
  • Role : Game Design and Graphism
