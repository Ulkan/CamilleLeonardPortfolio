About

Hi !

As you could guess from the title, my name is Camille Léonard and I'm a Level Designer and Level Builder.

I'm 28 years old and I'm a french men living in Paris

Graduated from a Bachelor in Game Design from ICAN in 2017.

I've got some professional experiences as a QA Tester and Webmaster but mostly as Level Designer and Level Builder.

Passionate with architecture, art and narrative design, I enjoy working on rich environments on the gameplay, level building and narration.

I favor RPG and actions games but I also enjoy strategy and management games. I like the Souls games, the Elder Scrolls Games and the MMO Guild Wars 2.

Actually working as a Level builder / Level Designer (both hats) at Cyanide Studio.

I wish to work as a Level Designer for RPG, action and adventure games.

I like to build level that the player want to play and who tell us story. The architectural and story-telling is an important part of my work and I'd like to work in many games companies.
